Mashed potatoes

A simple, yet elegant, addition to any steak meal

A return to normalcy. An irishman's best friend. The right hand man to a ribeye. It has gone by many names, but the form stays true. The dish was here before you were born and will be here long after you are gone.

Ingredients:

  • 6 yukon gold potatoes
  • 2 sticks butter
  • Kosher salt and freshly cracked pepper to taste
  1. Peel and boil potatoes
  2. Pour water out, keeping potatoes in pot. Mash.
  3. While not fully smooth, dump in two sticks butter. Continue mashing until smooth
  4. Season to taste with salt and pepper. enjoy
