Peanut butter and jelly

A simple, yet elegant, snack for any age

Since the dawn of time the peanut butter and jelly has been there for men and women of all ages. Whether you like it with crust on or off, cut into triangles or untouched, it unites us all as human.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices White bread
  • 2 spoonfuls peanut butter
  • 2 spoonfuls jelly, any flavor
  1. Seperate slices of bread
  2. Spread jelly on one slice, peanut butter on the other
  3. Combine, and enjoy
